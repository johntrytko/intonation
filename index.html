Our Goals

Passion

We strive to provide the highest quality of original music, suitable for film, television, commercials, and any other work. We love what we do, and we want our customers to be just as pleased with what we create. If you have a style or sound that you want, we would be pleased to accommodate you.

Integration

Located in Boulder, Colorado in the shadow of the Flatirons, we work with industry professionals to integrate our music with your shots. This relation allows us an unparalleled advantage when it come to how our music fits your scene.

Flexibility

All of our music is original, which allows for unending flexibility when it comes to finding the right sound for you. Any change you can name, we can meet. We welcome the challenge to compose themed or music from uncommon genres.
